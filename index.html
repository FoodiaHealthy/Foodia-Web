Take a photo.
Know everything.

Point your camera at any meal and Foodia's AI instantly identifies the dish, detects visible ingredients, estimates the full recipe, and delivers a complete nutritional breakdown — calories, macros, micros, and NutriScore rating.

  • Photo, text, or barcode scanner input
  • Automatic dish detection with confidence score

⚡ Exercise AI

Log workouts
in plain English.

No more tedious manual logging. Just describe your session the way you'd tell a friend — "4 sets bench press 80 kg × 10, 30 min running" — and Foodia's AI parses each exercise individually and calculates calories burned using MET values.

  • Natural language workout input
  • Detects each exercise separately
  • Calorie burn via MET × bodyweight
  • Review and remove incorrect entries
  • Full exercise history with editable names
