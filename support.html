General
Foodia is a nutrition and fitness tracking app powered by artificial intelligence. You can log your meals by photographing your food, scanning barcodes, or entering them manually. The app also tracks your exercise, water intake, and shows your nutritional history and progress over time.
Yes, Foodia is free to download and use. The free version includes core features with advertisements. Foodia Pro is a paid subscription that removes ads and unlocks unlimited AI analyses.
Foodia is currently available in 6 languages: English, Spanish, German, French, Italian, and Portuguese.

AI Features
Take a photo of your food and Foodia's AI (powered by Google Gemini) will identify the dish, estimate ingredients, and calculate approximate calories and macronutrients. The more clearly the food is visible in the photo, the more accurate the result.
AI estimates are approximations based on visual analysis. Results may vary depending on portion size, cooking method, and photo quality. We recommend using barcode scanning or manual entry for packaged foods where precise values are needed. Foodia is not a medical device and should not replace professional dietary advice.
The AI assistant can answer questions about nutrition, calories, macros, and healthy habits. It provides general informational responses and does not offer personalised medical or clinical advice.
Free users have a limited number of AI analyses per day. Foodia Pro users have unlimited access to all AI features.
Barcode Scanner & Foodia Score
Point your camera at any product barcode and Foodia will retrieve its nutritional information from our food database. The result is added directly to your food diary.
The NutriScore is a rating (A to E) that evaluates the nutritional quality of a food, taking into account factors such as calorie density, sugar content, saturated fats, protein, and fibre. It helps you make more informed food choices at a glance.
If a product is not in our database, you can log it manually by entering the nutritional values from the product label. You can also contact us at foodiahealthy@gmail.com and we will do our best to add it.
Foodia Pro Subscription
Foodia Pro removes all advertisements and unlocks:
  • Unlimited AI meal analyses
  • Unlimited AI chat with your nutritionist
  • Unlimited characters in questions and corrections
  • Export your full nutritional history to PDF with images
You can subscribe from within the app. Monthly ($5.99/month) and annual ($59.99/year) plans are available. Payment is processed securely through Apple App Store.
You can cancel anytime through your iPhone Settings: go to Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > Foodia Pro > Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period and you will keep access to Pro features until then.
First, try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, go to the subscription screen inside the app and tap "Restore Purchases". If you still have problems, contact us at foodiahealthy@gmail.com with your Apple ID and we will help you.
Refunds are handled by Apple. You can request a refund at reportaproblem.apple.com. We do not process refunds directly.
Data & Privacy
All your personal data — food diary, exercise logs, profile details, and nutritional history — is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or store this data on our servers.
Uninstalling the app removes your data from the device. iOS may retain some cached data for a period of time in case you reinstall the app. This is managed by iOS, not by Foodia.
We only collect your approximate location (if you grant permission) for geographic analytics. We do not sell your data. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for full details.
Technical Issues
Try the following steps:
  • Close the app completely and reopen it.
  • Restart your device.
  • Make sure you are running the latest version of Foodia from the App Store.
  • Make sure your iOS is up to date.
If the problem persists, contact us at foodiahealthy@gmail.com with your device model, iOS version, and a description of the issue.
Make sure the food is well-lit and clearly visible in the photo. Avoid blurry images or photos with multiple overlapping dishes. If the AI cannot identify the food, you can add it manually or via barcode scan.
Go to your Profile section and select the export option. You can generate a PDF report of your nutritional data and history directly from the app. This feature requires a Foodia Pro subscription.
